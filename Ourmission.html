Our Mission

To uplift tribal communities by providing them fair trade for their handmade art, while funding essential awareness programs, safety workshops, and sustainable development in remote schools.

What We Do

Fair Trade with Artisans

We buy clay toys and traditional crafts directly from tribal artisans at fair, respectful prices so their work is valued with dignity.

Safety & Awareness Programs

Donations help us organise awareness visits, safety training, and emergency education sessions for children in remote areas.

Direct Community Support

Funds are allocated for school supplies, small grants, and initiatives that directly benefit tribal families and their livelihood.

Why It Matters

Tribal communities hold rich art, culture, and knowledge. Yet many face economic instability and lack access to safety education. VanaMala bridges this gap by ensuring artisans earn fairly while children receive life-changing support.
