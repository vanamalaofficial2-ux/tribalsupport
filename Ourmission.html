Our Mission

Every year, tens of thousands of people lose their lives in disasters around the world — and many of those lives are taken in small, remote communities that have little or no safety training. Globally disasters have caused on average about 40,000–50,000 deaths per year in recent decades. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

In India alone recent government figures show that extreme natural hazards caused over 3,000 deaths in 2024–25 — most of them in rural and remote areas where people work outdoors and schools lack basic disaster awareness. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

We buy clay toys and crafts at fair prices so tribal artists earn more — and we use the profits to go into these communities and teach simple, life-saving safety steps: how to act during an earthquake, how to find safe exits during a fire, and how to prepare for floods. Small knowledge can save whole families.

When you buy from VanaMala, you are not only taking home a beautiful craft — you are helping a teacher reach a child who might otherwise never learn these simple rules.
